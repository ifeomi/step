Playlist Generator

Music’s ability to simultaneously connect people, express raw emotions, and carry political messages is thrilling to me. I really value discovering new artists and sharing songs with loved ones. I made this playlist generator because I love putting my library on shuffle and discovering unexpected song pairings from artists with different sounds. To use: choose some genres* and the number of songs and you’ll get a random playlist with songs from my library.

* I recognize that genres are flawed categories, and have in the past and the present been problematically racialized, so these categorizations are an imperfect work in progress.
